The Championship

One campus. Every arena.

Pravah 2026 puts esports, sports and cultural competition on the same three-day bracket. From 15–17 July, SKIT and delegations from other colleges compete across digital arenas, playing fields and the main stage — one tournament ecosystem, one campus, one championship.

Queue into a squad, sign up solo, or bring your whole team — every arena has its own venue, coordinator and prize pool, tracked live throughout the festival.
